Project Narrative

The primary objective of this project was to undertake a comprehensive landscape renovation, transforming the property into a functional and aesthetically harmonious “Desert Modernist” outdoor living space. The backyard underwent a redesign to serve as an extension of the home, incorporating a comprehensive outdoor kitchen (equipped with a grill, pizza oven, and sink), a pool and spa area, and multiple distinct seating and dining zones.

The design incorporates iconic desert modern elements, notably a double-sided fireplace with breeze block accents and masonry benches with a stucco finish. The front yard renovation involved the installation of a new 5-foot-tall stuccoed block wall and a contemporary entry gate to enhance privacy and delineate the property boundary.

Implementing a Desert-Adapted Landscape: The plant selection prioritizes drought-resistant, architectural species such as Yucca Rostrata, Hercules Aloe, and Blue Barrel Cactus, all situated within a structured ground cover of multi-colored crushed rock. This transformation expanded the usable living space and enhanced the privacy, functionality, and aesthetic value of the property.
