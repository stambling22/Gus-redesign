Capabilities

Our Services

Delivering high-end landscape architecture tailored specifically to the arid climate of the desert.

01 /

Residential Design + Build

We transform residential backyards into modernist desert oases. Our designs seamlessly extend the interior of the home into the landscape, incorporating high-quality materials and drought-resistant flora.

  • Premium hardscaping (Porcelain Pavers & Sand-Finish Concrete)
  • Pool & spa renovation with custom stone coping
  • Outdoor kitchens, fire bowls, and breeze block dividers
  • Drought-tolerant plants (Agaves, Cacti, Olive Trees)
  • Smart drip irrigation & low-voltage landscape lighting
02 /

Commercial & HOA Stewardship

Dedicated pathway for commercial developers, retail plazas, luxury hospitality venues, and HOA communities. We provide structured landscape design, execution, and regular maintenance to sustain property asset value.

  • HOA common area landscape design & renovation
  • Water management audit & irrigation modernization
  • Commercial property cleanup, styling, and planting
  • Reliable contract maintenance with fast response times
  • Compliance with local desert water conservation policies

Methodology

Our Process

Our structured three-step delivery journey ensures projects are delivered on schedule and within budget.

01.

Design

We create a comprehensive spatial plan, including detailed plant lists, materials selection, and 3D concept renderings to preview the final layout.

02.

Installation

Our skilled construction team undertakes hardscape layout, masonry work, masonry finish, planting, water management, and low-voltage electrical systems.

03.

Maintenance

Post-installation care tailored to ensure that architectural flora and delicate desert plantings thrive while keeping irrigation settings optimized.

Our Story

Licensed Landscape Stewardship

Led by Gus Diaz, **Gus Landscape Contractors Inc.** has been crafting high-end residential and commercial landscapes in the Coachella Valley for over 5 years. As a licensed California C27 contractor, we understand the delicate balance of desert soils, microclimates, and water efficiency.
