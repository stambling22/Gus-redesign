Project Narrative

The objective of this project was a complete landscape renovation for a residence in Indian Wells. The project aimed to transform the property's backyard into a modern outdoor living and entertainment space. The layout features significant updates to the pool and spa, surrounded by structured hardscapes.

Entertainment zones were carefully integrated into the design, including a fire pit seating area and a custom 4K covered patio. The design utilized a low-maintenance palette of desert-adapted plants, such as Yucca rostrata, various structural cacti (San Pedro, Golden Barrel, Totem Pole), and architectural Agaves (Artichoke, Blue Glow).

To achieve a clean, contemporary look, the renovation combined sand-finish concrete and a new seat wall with artificial turf segments and "Chamois Beige" crushed rock mulch. Ultimately, the renovation sought to balance aesthetic appeal with high functionality, providing the homeowners with multiple outdoor areas for relaxation and social gatherings.
