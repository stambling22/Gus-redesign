Project Narrative

This Palm Springs project transforms a residential backyard into a modern desert oasis, seamlessly blending mid-century modern architecture with functional outdoor living spaces. The clean, geometric layout is defined by porcelain pavers and acid wash concrete, creating distinct zones for relaxation and entertainment. This design balances low-maintenance desert materials with lush, functional areas.

The renovation introduces multiple destination spots within the backyard. The existing curved pool and spa are refreshed with acid-washed concrete coping and surrounded by porcelain paver decking, perfect for sunbathing. A dedicated seating area features a large Corten steel fire bowl, while an outdoor shower area is screened by breeze block.

The planting strategy combines existing mature greenery with new, structurally significant desert flora. Architectural succulents like Yucca rostrata, Madagascar Palms, and an Olive tree are incorporated into the design.

Project Details

  • Client: Private Residence
  • Location: Palm Springs, CA
  • Hardscape: Porcelain Pavers, Acid-Wash Concrete
  • Special Features: Corten Fire Bowl, Stucco Benches, Breeze Block Screen
