Project Narrative

The primary objective of this project was to create a harmonious and multi-functional outdoor space for relaxation and entertainment. The design direction is inspired by the “Palm Springs Modern” aesthetic, characterized by a neutral palette of whites and greys in the hardscape, which provides a sophisticated backdrop for vibrant pops of color. The project incorporates architectural elements like mid-century-inspired breeze block walls to define distinct “rooms” within the yard while maintaining an open and airy atmosphere.

The site features high-quality materials, including 24”x48” Porcelain Pavers and natural grey concrete with a sand finish. The design centers around an existing pool and spa, enhanced with a raised bond beam featuring mitered edge stone coping and new tile. Outdoor living amenities include a BBQ island with a waterfall counter and decorative blue-and-white wave tiling, an Alumawood shade structure equipped with recessed lighting, ceiling fans, and a misting system for year-round comfort. A Lumacast fire pit with a white aesthetic complements the surrounding pavers.

The plant palette consists of drought-tolerant species such as Golden Barrel Cactus, Agave (Blue Flame and Octopus), Totem Pole Cactus, and Date Palms. These are balanced with artificial turf areas used for lawn games and visual softening. Architectural details include a modern 5-foot-high breeze block wall that serves as functional dividers and privacy screens. Decorative metal screens are used to conceal utility areas like air conditioning units while adding a decorative element.
